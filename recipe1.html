Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until it is cooked but still firm.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce,salt and pepper.Cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and serve.

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
