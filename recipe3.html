Ingredients

  • Vegetable balls (cabbage, carrot, flour)
  • 2 tablespoon Soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Spring onions

Instructions

  1. Mix vegetables with flour and cornflour,shape into balls and fry.
  2. Cook sauce with soy, garlic, ginger.
  3. Add fried balls to the sauce and garnish with spring onions.

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
