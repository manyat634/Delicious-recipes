Carbonara Ramen

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen noodles
  • Egg yolk
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook ramen noodles.
  2. Mix egg yolk and cheese.
  3. Add noodles, bacon, and pepper.
  4. Serve creamy and hot.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
