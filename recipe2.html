Kadhi Chawal

  • Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • Spices (turmeric, chili, cumin)
  • Cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt and gram flour with water to make a smooth mixture.
  2. Add spices and simmer until thick and creamy.
  3. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Prep Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
